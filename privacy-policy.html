Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

Welcome to NutriScan ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

⚡ Quick Summary: We collect health and nutrition data to provide personalized nutrition insights. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, height, weight, activity level, dietary preferences
  • Health Information: Meal photos, food logs, symptoms, wellness scores, nutrient deficiencies
  • Photos: Meal images, body part images (skin, nails, eyes, tongue, hair) for visual analysis
  • Payment Information: Billing details for premium subscriptions (processed by Stripe)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, scan frequency
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Country/region (for leaderboard rankings) - we do NOT track precise location
  • Analytics: App performance, crash reports, user behavior patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Analyze meals, detect nutrient deficiencies, calculate wellness scores
  • AI Processing: Send meal/body photos to OpenAI GPT-4 Vision for analysis
  • Personalization: Customize recommendations based on your health profile
  • Leaderboards: Display your wellness score on global/country leaderboards
  • Referrals: Track referral codes and rewards
  • Achievements: Award badges and track progress
  • Payment Processing: Process premium subscriptions via Stripe
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and promotional content (you can opt out)
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws and prevent fraud/abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers:
    • Supabase (database hosting)
    • OpenAI (AI meal/visual analysis)
    • Stripe (payment processing)
    • Cloud storage providers (image hosting)
    • Analytics providers (app performance)
  • Public Leaderboards: Display name and wellness score (not email or personal details)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 🔒 Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • 🔐 Database Security: Row-Level Security (RLS) in Supabase
  • 🗝️ Password Protection: Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • ☁️ Secure Storage: Images stored in private buckets with signed URLs
  • 🛡️ Access Controls: Limited employee access to user data
⚠️ Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your nutrition data in CSV/JSON format (premium feature)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop AI analysis or visual scanning anytime

To exercise these rights: Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Rights, or email us at privacy@nutriscan.app

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with laws
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely

7. Children's Privacy

NutriScan is NOT intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in:

  • United States (Supabase, OpenAI, Stripe servers)
  • European Union (Supabase EU regions)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with GDPR and other regulations.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • OpenAI: Meal and visual analysis - see OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing - see Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Database and authentication - see Supabase Privacy Policy

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication, session management
  • Analytics Cookies: App usage tracking (anonymized)
  • NO Advertising Cookies: We don't track you for ads

You can manage cookie preferences in app Settings → Privacy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal data we collect and why
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Opt-Out: We don't sell data, so no opt-out needed
  • Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising rights

Contact us at privacy@nutriscan.app to exercise these rights.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on consent and legitimate interests
  • Data Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Automated Decision-Making: AI analysis is not used for solely automated decisions affecting you legally

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Major changes: We'll notify you via email or in-app notification.
