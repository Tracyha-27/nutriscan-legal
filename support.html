🆘 Quick Help

❓ How do I scan a meal?

1. Tap the Scan button on the home screen
2. Take a photo of your meal or select from gallery
3. Wait 5-10 seconds for AI analysis
4. Review detected foods and nutrition
5. Edit if needed, then save

💎 How do I upgrade to Premium?

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Choose Monthly ($7.99) or Yearly ($59.99)
3. Tap "Start 7-Day Free Trial"
4. Confirm payment through Google Play
5. Enjoy unlimited scans!

❌ How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open Google Play Store
2. Tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
3. Find "NutriScan Premium"
4. Tap "Cancel subscription"
5. Access continues until end of billing period

🎁 How does the referral program work?

1. Go to Profile → Referrals
2. Share your unique referral code
3. Friends sign up using your code
4. Earn rewards:
• 3 referrals = 1 month free
• 10 referrals = 6 months free
• 25 referrals = Lifetime free!

🏆 How do I climb the leaderboard?

• Log meals daily (consistency matters!)
• Maintain high wellness scores (80+)
• Build streak days
• Meet nutrient goals
• Complete achievements

📊 What is Wellness Score?

Your wellness score (0-100) is calculated based on:
• Nutrient intake quality
• Calorie balance
• Micronutrient coverage
• Consistency of logging
• Deficiency levels

🔬 How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI achieves 85-95% accuracy for common foods. Accuracy improves with:
• Good lighting in photos
• Clear view of all food items
• Standard portions
Always review and edit results for best accuracy.

📵 Does it work offline?

• Food logging: Yes, syncs when online
• Food database: Yes, cached locally
• AI meal scanning: No, requires internet
• Leaderboard: No, requires internet

🔒 How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion
⚠️ This permanently deletes all your data within 30 days.

📥 Can I export my data?

Premium users only:
1. Go to Settings → Data
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Choose format (CSV or JSON)
4. Download to your device

🐛 Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • Update to latest version in Google Play
  • Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → NutriScan → Clear Cache
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app (data is saved in cloud)

AI scan not working

  • Check internet connection
  • Ensure good lighting in photo
  • Try taking photo again
  • Check if you've reached free tier limit (7 scans/week)

Can't log in

  • Verify email and password are correct
  • Check for typos in email
  • Try "Forgot Password" to reset
  • Check internet connection

Subscription not activating

  • Wait 5-10 minutes for processing
  • Log out and log back in
  • Restore purchase: Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchase
  • Contact support if issue persists

Foods not appearing in search

  • Check spelling
  • Try simpler search terms (e.g., "chicken" instead of "grilled chicken breast")
  • Check internet connection (for full database)
  • Create custom food if not found
